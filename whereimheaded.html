Where I'm Headed

1. What I’m currently up to

Currently, I'm on a coding quest with HyperionDev, mastering FullStack Web and Software Engineering. Think of it like a digital adventure, where I'm leveling up my skills one line of code at a time! If you're ready to dive into the coding cosmos, HyperionDev is an amazing place to do this.

2. Helping start-up and small Businesses build an online presence

After graduating from HyperionDev, my sights are set on empowering start-up and small businesses to thrive in the digital world. Through personalised consultations and tailored solutions, I want to aid them into their online presence, crafting websites and platforms that not only showcase their brand but also drive growth and engagement. This includes but is not limited to e-commerce storefronts & interactive portfolios, I'd like to be the catalyst for their digital success.

3. Design & develop software to create efficiency and help NPO's

My focus? Designing and developing software solutions to streamline operations and amplify impact for non-profit organisations. This would consist of donor management systems to volunteer coordination platforms, I want to be able to empower NPOs with the tools they need to maximise their resources and make a lasting difference in the communities they serve.

4. Design & develop software to create efficiency for businesses in the Renewable Energy sector

We’re in dire need of sustainable solutions, with that, my vision extends to the renewable energy sector. I aspire to design and develop software that enhances efficiency and innovation for businesses in this industry. From energy management systems to streamlining renewable energy production processes, I want to be able to drive progress towards a greener, more sustainable future.
