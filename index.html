Here’s a little bit about me:

I’m a born-and-bred South African with a journey as colourful as the country itself. I started off in the heart of Cape Town, soaked up the energy of Johannesburg during my childhood, and now I've made my way back to the Mother City for round two. Let me tell you, the beauty here is genuinely irresistible – the Mountain views, the ocean breeze, and yes, even the unpredictable weather. I adore rainy days, but these winters are a whole new level of wet. I’m still in the process of figuring out how to enjoy them without turning into a human prune and getting laundry done.

Academically, I’ve always been a bit of a geek. I trained as an electronics technician, diving into the nuts and bolts of the tech world. In 2022, I leveled up with a degree in Information Management – yep, I’m all about that data life. Now, I’m channeling my techy background and love for innovation into the renewable energy sector as a junior project manager. It's thrilling to be part of an industry that's shaping the future and saving the planet one solar panel at a time.

So, whether I’m chasing the sun or dancing in the rain, I’m all about embracing the journey and making the most of every moment here in the Cape.
